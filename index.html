About StartSite

StartSite is a modern web studio focused on delivering premium single-page websites that combine luxury aesthetics with practical performance. We build sites that help small and mid-sized businesses look trustworthy, professional, and conversion-ready.

Our process blends strategic UX decisions, refined visual design, and lean, well-documented code. We design with purpose: clear messaging, strong CTAs, and layouts that guide visitors to act. We also include guidance for GitHub Pages deployment so non-technical founders can publish with confidence.

Every project receives hand-crafted design touches (typography, spacing, visual rhythm), responsive optimization, and optional accessibility improvements. We prioritize clarity, speed, and a polished brand presentation at affordable price tiers.

How It Works — 3 Steps

1 — Discovery & Goals

We discuss your business, audience and key goals. We'll propose the site structure and main messaging points so the final product aligns to outcomes.

  • 30-minute discovery call
  • Clear deliverables & timeline

2 — Design & Build

We craft a focused one-page layout with premium typography, refined spacing, and smooth micro-animations. Development uses clean HTML/CSS/JS so files are easy to maintain.

  • Responsive across devices
  • Performance & SEO basics

3 — Launch & Handover

We perform final QA, deliver optimized assets, and provide step-by-step publishing instructions (GitHub Pages). Optional maintenance and support packages are available.

  • 1–3 day typical turnaround
  • Deployment guidance & handover notes
